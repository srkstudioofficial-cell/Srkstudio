Questions & Answers

Kya aap Bihar ke bahar bhi shoot karte hain? ▼
Bilkul! Hum poore India mein wedding shoots karte hain — Bihar, Jharkhand, UP, Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore. Travel & stay charges actual basis pe additional honge.
Booking confirm karne ke liye kitna advance dena padega? ▼
Date confirm karne ke liye total package ka 30% advance lagta hai (online ya cash). Baki amount delivery se pehle. UPI: 9110066743@ybl (Meena Devi — PhonePe).
Kitne din mein photos aur video deliver karte ho? ▼
Highlight reel: 7 din. Edited photos (200-500): 15-20 din. Cinematic film + full album: 30 din maximum. Sab cloud aur pen drive mein milta hai.
Kya raw footage milti hai? ▼
Haan, sab raw photos pen drive mein delivered hote hain. Edited high-quality versions Google Drive pe bhi share karte hain — lifetime access.
Drone shots aur cinematic video sab packages mein available hai? ▼
Drone shots A+, A, B, C packages mein included hain. Cinematic film sab packages mein hai. Custom requirements ke liye baat kar sakte hain.
Pre-wedding aur engagement shoots karte ho? ▼
Bilkul! Pre-wedding, engagement, haldi, mehendi, sangeet — sab functions cover karte hain. Standalone pre-wedding shoot Rs 25,000 se shuru.
